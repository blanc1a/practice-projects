Patrick Swayze

American actor, dancer and singer known for playing distinctive lead roles, particularly romantic, tough and comedic characters.

Also known for his media image and looks.

About his life:

Patrick Wayne Swayze was born on August 18, 1952, in Houston, Texas. He was the son of a dancer, choreographer and dance instructor, and an engineering draftman. He had an older sister and two younger brothers. He started off his career as a dancer for the Disney Theatrical Group in a show called Disney on Parade. 1983 he became better known to the film industry after appearing in The Outsiders as the older brother of C. Thomas Howell and Rob Lowe. He later starred in the low-budget movie Dirty Dancing which became a surprise hit, and then achievied enormous international success. He was married to Lisa Niemi for 34 years until his death. He died at the age of 57 surrounded by his family 20 month after his cancer diagnosis.
